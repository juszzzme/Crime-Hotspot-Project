Crime Prevention & Safety Tips

Your personal safety is our priority. Learn essential tips and strategies to protect yourself, your family, and your property in various situations.

Home Security

Securing Your Home

  • Install quality locks on all doors and windows
  • Use timers on lights when away from home
  • Don't hide spare keys in obvious places
  • Keep bushes and trees trimmed near windows
  • Consider a home security system
Personal Safety

Personal Safety Tips

  • Stay alert and aware of your surroundings
  • Avoid walking alone at night in unfamiliar areas
  • Keep valuables out of sight in your vehicle
  • Have your keys ready when approaching your car or home
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, leave
Digital Safety

Staying Safe Online

  • Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts
  • Enable two-factor authentication where available
  • Be cautious about sharing personal information online
  • Keep your devices and software updated
  • Learn to recognize phishing attempts
Emergency

Emergency Preparedness

  • Create a family emergency plan
  • Keep emergency contact numbers handy
  • Know the emergency exits in buildings you frequent
  • Learn basic first aid and CPR
  • Keep an emergency kit with essential supplies
